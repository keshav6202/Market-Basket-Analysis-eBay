# Market Basket Analysis & Customer Behavior Insights - eBay

A comprehensive customer analytics and recommendation project that analyzes eBay customer purchasing behavior, product preferences, browsing patterns, satisfaction levels, and recommendation effectiveness using Python, Machine Learning, and Data Visualization.

## Project Summary

This project focuses on understanding customer shopping behavior and identifying opportunities for personalized recommendations, customer segmentation, and business optimization. The analysis combines customer demographics, purchase frequency, browsing habits, review engagement, and satisfaction metrics to generate actionable business insights.

The system supports:

* Customer demographic analysis
* Purchase behavior analysis
* Product category trend identification
* Customer segmentation and profiling
* Recommendation effectiveness analysis
* Shopping satisfaction evaluation
* Market Basket Analysis and association rule mining
* Business intelligence reporting and visualization

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Removed duplicate records
* Handled missing values
* Standardized categorical variables
* Corrected inconsistent column names
* Converted rating fields into numerical format
* Prepared data for analysis and modeling

### 2. Descriptive Behavior Analysis

* Gender distribution analysis
* Age group analysis
* Purchase frequency trends
* Popular product category identification
* Browsing behavior evaluation
* Cart abandonment analysis
* Customer satisfaction assessment

### 3. Customer Segmentation

Customers were categorized into behavioral groups:

#### Frequent Buyers

* High purchase frequency
* High satisfaction level

#### Occasional Shoppers

* Moderate purchasing activity
* Average satisfaction

#### At-Risk Customers

* Low satisfaction scores
* Frequent cart abandonment

K-Means clustering was applied to identify hidden customer segments.

### 4. Recommendation & Review Insights

* Relationship between recommendation usefulness and satisfaction
* Impact of customer reviews on purchasing decisions
* Trust level in personalized recommendations
* Recommendation system improvement opportunities

### 5. Data Visualization

Created visual reports including:

* Purchase category distribution
* Customer satisfaction analysis
* Customer segmentation charts
* Correlation heatmaps
* Purchase frequency distributions
* Recommendation effectiveness visualizations
